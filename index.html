About Startup

Meet Pivo, a platform that centralizes the way freight carriers collect, make and track payments. Starting with freight carriers, Pivo provides easy access to a smart bank account, debit card and digital invoicing tools that track payments so they can get paid faster. Impacted Users would be anyone who is looking for a platform that organizes all the financial aspects of their supply chain business in one place. With our new interface these users will be able to navigate through and manage their day-to-day financial operations with ease.

User Testing Videos

Users were asked to:

  • Login
  • Send a payment to josiah carberry
  • Go back to dashboard and check “account info”
  • Exit the app

Users had no problems completing login, payment, and exiting, but were confused about checking account info. The prototype does not have any elements that are labeled with exactly “account info”. The profile button in the top right corner and the “account details” button on the side menu. Both lead to the desired page, but it took the users some time to figure that out. One user wanted more clarity on how the contacts were ordered, and the option to change how they were ordered.

Overall, these results are in line with our expectations. The users had a generally easy time using the interface and gave good reviews. We do not want to change anything related to account info, the troubles there were more the fault of the wording of the question than the interface itself. Adding a way to sort contacts by recent, alphabetical, etc. would be a good addition.
